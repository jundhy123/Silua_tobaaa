## About Silua Toba

Silua Toba is a premium culinary platform dedicated to bringing the authentic tastes of the Batak heritage directly to your doorstep. We combine traditional family recipes with modern standards to ensure the highest quality and freshest ingredients from the Lake Toba region.

### Core Values
- **Heritage**: Preserving generations of Batak culinary traditions.
- **Quality**: Using only the finest, handcrafted ingredients.
- **Authenticity**: Honest flavors, no preservatives, and pure passion.

## Platform Features
- **Curated Catalog**: Explore our wide range of traditional sambals, snacks, and premium robusta coffee.
- **Secure Ordering**: Seamless integration with WhatsApp for personalized service.
- **Visual Archive**: A journey through our history and craftsmanship.
- **Customer Wall**: Honest testimonials from our community of food lovers.

## Technical Overview

Built with **Laravel 12** and **Tailwind CSS**, this platform features:
- Responsive Editorial Design
- Full Administrative Dashboard
- Secure User Authentication
- Product Inventory Management
- Real-time Order Tracking
